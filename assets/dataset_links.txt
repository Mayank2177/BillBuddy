1. Zenodo – Dataset of invoices and receipts (813 images, Portuguese, with key fields annotated like seller, tax IDs, date, totals)
  https://zenodo.org/records/6371710
2. High‑Quality Invoice Images for OCR (Kaggle)
  https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/osamahosamabdellatif/high-quality-invoice-images-for-ocr
3. FATURA: Multi‑Layout Invoice Image Dataset – 10,000 invoice images across 50 unique layouts
  https://arxiv.org/pdf/2311.11856.pdf
4. Samples of electronic invoices (Mendeley) – 1000 auto‑generated invoices with valid information
  https://data.mendeley.com/datasets/tnj49gpmtz/2
5. Kaggle “Invoices Dataset” (tabular) – Invoice‑like records with fields such as name, product ID, quantity, amount, invoice date, city, etc.
  https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/cankatsrc/invoices
6. Hugging Face – invoices‑donut‑data‑v1 – Donut‑style dataset prepared for document OCR/understanding models on invoices.
  https://huggingface.co/datasets/katanaml-org/invoices-donut-data-v1
7. Roboflow Universe – Invoice datasets & models – Multiple community invoice datasets and pre‑trained detection/OCR models for invoice numbers, shipment details, etc.
  https://universe.roboflow.com/browse/logistics/invoice
8. GitHub – invoice_dataset
  https://github.com/mouadhamri/invoice_dataset
9. UiPath forum – 1000+ PDF Invoice dataset 
  https://forum.uipath.com/t/1000-pdf-invoice-dataset-for-automation/562551
